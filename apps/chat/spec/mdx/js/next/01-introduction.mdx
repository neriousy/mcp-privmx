**Safe Notes** - app for creating **E2E Encrypted** notes. You will build the app with PrivMX and Next.js.

## Concepts

This tutorial will introduce the following concepts:

1. Setting up your local **PrivMX Bridge**
2. Working with the **@simplito/privmx-webendpoint** in your project
3. Threads
4. Event handling

## Prerequisites

1. Basic knowledge of JavaScript, [React](https://react.dev/) and [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/)
2. Have [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/en), [NPM](https://www.npmjs.com/) and [Docker](https://www.docker.com/) installed on your computer.
